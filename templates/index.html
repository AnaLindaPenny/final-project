OUR MARVEL-LOUS MISSION

    A POW-er stricken analysis of the box office performances of movies from the Marvel Cinematic Universe and DC Cinematic Universe. Data manipulation was demonstrated by the use of various comparisons that relay the success of individual superhero movie series (ex. THOR franchise vs. IRON MAN, etc.). Comparisons of success based off of lead roles factoring gender or race differences. We also used Machine Learning to predict the success of future movies; including the success of movies based off of which characters/actors are in the movies.
