Routes

Title Description Route
Clean Coasters Route will render a json of the clean roller coaster data, included completed fields for Roller Coaster Names, Amusement Park Name, Type, Design, Status, and Open Date. /api/coasters/

Title Description Route
Drops Route will render a table-formatted json of the top 10 roller coasters by depth of drop, park name, location, and ranking. /api/records/drop/
Heights Route will render a table-formatted json of the top 10 roller coasters by maximum height, park name, location, and ranking. /api/records/height/
Lengths Route will render a table-formatted json of the top 10 roller coasters by track length, park name, location, and ranking. /api/records/length/
Speeds Route will render a table-formatted json of the top 10 roller coasters by top speed, park name, location, and ranking. /api/records/speed/

Title Description Route
IMDb Route will render JSON of IMDb ratings. /api/imdb
Heights Route will render a geojson of the top 10 roller coasters by maximum height, park name, location (latitude and longitude), geometry, and ranking. /api/records/height_geo/
Lengths Route will render a geojson of the top 10 roller coasters by track length, park name, location (latitude and longitude), geometry, and ranking. /api/records/length_geo/
Speeds Route will render a geojson of the top 10 roller coasters by top speed, park name, location (latitude and longitude), geometry, and ranking. /api/records/speed_geo/
Worldview Route will render a geojson of the count of roller coasters per country, country's geometry, country's population and area (per square mile). /api/worldview/
